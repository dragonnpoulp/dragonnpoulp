Summary

About the Role
We are seeking an experienced Senior Full-Stack Developer for a targeted optimization contract to resolve specific backend performance and memory bottlenecks on our live staging environment.

Our application features data-heavy construction technology tools, estimating workflows, and architectural plan assets. Currently, specific modules suffer from V8 engine heap out-of-memory crashes caused by un-paginated eager-loading queries. The root files and exact database structures causing this issue have already been isolated. Your job will be to refactor the data flow from a "pull-everything" model to an efficient, server-side paginated and lazy-loaded structure.
